Setting up Email in Outlook 2010

  1. Start Outlook.
  2. Click the File tab
  3. In the Info category, click Account Settings, and then click Account Settings in the dropdown.
  4. On the Email tab, click New or click twice on Email Address to open its Internet Email Settings.
    • Follow these steps in the Add New E-mail Account dialog box:
      1. Click to select the Manually configure server settings or additional server types check box, and then click Next.
      2. Click Internet Email, and then click Next.
      3. Under User Information, follow these steps:
        1. In the Your Name box, type your full name.
        2. In the Email Address box, type your full e-mail address.
      4. Under Server Information
        • Choose POP3 in the Account Type box, follow these steps:
          1. In the Incoming mail server box, type "mail.domain.com".
          2. In the Outgoing mail server (SMTP) box, type "mail.domain.com".
      5. Under Logon Information, follow these steps:
        1. In the User Name box, type your full e-mail address.
        2. In the Password box, type the password provided.
        3. If you want Outlook to remember your email account password, click to select the Remember password check box.
      6. Click More Settings to open the Internet Email Settings dialog box.
        1. On the Outgoing Server tab, click on My Outgoing Server (SMTP) requires authentication to perform a check next to it.
      7. Click OK then Next, and then click Finish.
    You're all set!
