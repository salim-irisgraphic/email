Setting up Email on Android

  1. Locate and open your Email Client Application

    2. In the Select Email Provider screen, Press on Other

  2. Type in your Email Address and Password, then press on Manual Setup

    3. In the Select Account Type screen:

  3. Select POP3 and press Next
  4. In the Incoming Server Settings enter your POP3 server, Username, Password
  5. In the Outgoing Server Settings enter your SMTP server, Username, Password
  6. After you press Next The account will verify its settings.
  7. Enter your Description and press on Done to Finish.
    You're all set!
