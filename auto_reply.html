How to Setup Auto Reply on Outlook

  1. First of all you need to Create a new Email

  2. Go to File -> Save As and choose the "Save as: Outlook Template"

  3. Close the New Email Window and Go To File -> Rules and Alerts ->"E-mail Rules" -> New Rule check the "Apply rule on messages I receive Like the image below:

  4. Click "Next" under "Step 1" and hit "Yes"

  5. Check the box that says: "reply using a specific template"

  6. Under Step 2, Click on the underlined a specific template and choose your template Saved from Point #2

  7. Rename the Rule for easier access and click "Finish"

  8. Tick the checkbox next to the Rule's name uner "Rules and Alerts" and click "Apply"

    You're all set!
